Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our social media automation application ("the App") handles information when connecting to Facebook and other social media platforms for automated content posting purposes.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed solely for automated content distribution to social media platforms on behalf of our clients. We collect and process only the following information:

  • Facebook Business Page Access: Authorization tokens to post content to designated business pages
  • Content Data: Text, images, and videos that are scheduled for posting
  • Posting Metadata: Scheduling information and posting status

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information from end users or page visitors
  • Browsing history or tracking data
  • Any data beyond what's necessary for content posting

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Publishing scheduled content to authorized social media pages
  • Maintaining posting schedules and automation workflows
  • Ensuring successful content delivery

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is processed through secure automation workflows using n8n and stored temporarily only as needed for content distribution. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect access tokens and content data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any information with third parties except:

  • The social media platforms themselves (Facebook, Instagram, etc.) as necessary to post content
  • AI services used for content generation (when applicable)

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following platforms to provide automation services:

  • Facebook Business API
  • Instagram API
  • Other social media platform APIs as configured
  • Content generation services (AI text, image, and video tools)

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

7. User Rights

As a business page administrator who has authorized our App:

  • You can revoke access at any time through Facebook Business Manager
  • You can request deletion of any stored content or access tokens
  • You maintain full control over your social media accounts

8. Data Retention

We retain content and access credentials only as long as necessary to provide the automation service. Upon service termination or access revocation, all related data is deleted.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for business use only and does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Compliance

This App complies with:

  • Facebook Platform Policies
  • Meta Business Tools Terms
  • Applicable data protection regulations
